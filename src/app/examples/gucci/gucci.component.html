Guccio Gucci

Formation


Gucci was born on March 26, 1881 in Florence, the son of an Italian merchant from the North of Italy. He founded Gucci in Florence in 1906, when it was just a small shop selling leather goods. From the very beginning of his career, he was interested in hiring the best craftsmen he could find to work in the workshop. He began selling leather bags to equestrians in the 1920s and quickly built his reputation for quality[1] at a very young age.For the last several years of his life, Guccio Gucci lived near Rusper, West Sussex, England. He died on January 2, 1953 in Florence, Italy.
